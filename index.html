Scary Susan

This is Scary Susan. She only comes out when there is a full moon. Her pet pumpkin keeps watch for food for her. Once spotted, Scary Susan will eat the food. No on has ever escaped her.
Scary Susan loves Halloween. This is because there is so much candy. Around this time, candy is the only food Scary Susan will eat. Her favorite candy is Twizzlers. She also loves the costumes she sees at night.
Her et pumpkin also loves Halloween because he can see all of his pumpkin friends. He is sad when Halloween is over because he can't see them until the next Halloween.
